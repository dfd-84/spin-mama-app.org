Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how our website and apps use small data files to make core features work properly. It applies whenever you visit, sign in, or interact with our services on web or mobile. The policy also outlines your choices and how you can control technologies that store or access information on your device. We aim to be transparent about the tools we use, why we use them, and how long we keep related data. These explanations are tailored to typical scenarios you may encounter while using spinmama services. If you still have questions after reading, you can contact us using the details provided below, including matters related to spin mama settings.

Summary of the purpose of the cookie policy

Cookies help us provide reliable sign-in, remember preferences, and understand how people use key screens. Without them, essential features such as session management and fraud prevention would not function as intended. We also use them to measure performance so we can fix problems and improve navigation. Where marketing cookies are involved, we request consent when required by law and honor your choices. These purposes are limited to what we need to operate and improve experiences connected to spin mama, and they never change how you interact with spinmama content without your control.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your browser by websites you visit. Some last only while your browser is open, and others persist for a defined period so preferences can be remembered. Similar technologies like local storage, pixels, and SDKs perform comparable functions on apps and may be treated the same way in this policy. First-party cookies are set by us, while third-party cookies come from partners that provide services like analytics. Each cookie has a name, value, and expiration, and it is scoped to a domain and path. We group them by function so you can see which ones are required for spinmama features and which enhance the experience around spin mama.

How and why cookies are used

We categorize our cookies to make choices clear and manageable. Essential cookies keep the service secure and stable, while functional cookies remember settings such as language or accessibility preferences. Analytics cookies help us understand what is working and what needs attention, informing product decisions. Where permitted, advertising cookies support relevant messaging and limit repetition across sessions. Your browser or device may also store identifiers used by our mobile SDKs so spin mama actions can be measured responsibly for spinmama improvements. We use the following categories in everyday operation to keep things predictable and understandable for you:

  • Essential: used for authentication, session continuity, security checks, and load balancing.

  • Functional: remember preferences like region, language, and accessibility choices.

  • Analytics: help us measure visits, diagnose errors, and improve features over time.

  • Advertising/measurement: where allowed, support relevance, capping, and campaign reporting.

  • Performance: assess speed, responsiveness, and stability across different devices.

These categories are designed to give you control without breaking key features. You can manage cookies through our settings panel or your browser controls at any time. When you disable a category, we stop setting new cookies in that category and make reasonable efforts to remove nonessential ones already set. Essential cookies will continue because parts of the site cannot function without them. We review our partners periodically and update this page to reflect active integrations. Retention varies by cookie type, and we set durations that are proportionate to the stated purpose; wherever possible, we favor shorter lifetimes and aggregate reporting to protect spinmama users while keeping spin mama services usable.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have legal rights regarding the use of cookies and related personal data. These can include the right to withdraw consent, object to certain processing, and request information about the data collected. We respect these rights and provide accessible ways to exercise them. Some rights apply universally, while others apply only in specific jurisdictions under local law. The steps below summarize practical actions you can take when using spinmama, regardless of whether you access the service on web or mobile.

  1. Adjust cookie categories in your account or browser settings to control nonessential technologies.

  2. Withdraw consent for marketing or analytics where consent is the legal basis, and we will honor that choice.

  3. Request access to information associated with identifiers used by cookies or similar technologies.

  4. Ask for correction or deletion of personal data where applicable under the law.

  5. Object to processing based on legitimate interests and request restriction in specific circumstances.

  6. Lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority if you believe your rights are not respected.

If you exercise any of these options, core features should still work, though personalization may be reduced. Verification may be required to protect your account before we act on sensitive requests. We aim to respond within a reasonable period and will explain any legal limits that restrict us from fulfilling a request. For app environments, controls may appear in the device’s privacy settings as well as our in-app menu. Should you need help choosing settings, you can write to us and mention your spin mama account so we can locate records related to spinmama usage.
